CATALOGING

All materials located in this location do not circulate.

Extensive collection of over 200 titles is used as tools for cataloging Jenkins collection as well as collections of many Philadelphia's law firms. Jenkins catalogs materials of firms, which are part of the Processing Center.

Processing Center is an initiative started by Jenkins in 1987 and provides wide varieties of technical support and expertise to local firm librarians.

To become a member of our Processing Center contact Linda Quick at 215-574-7956.
